Report Date 27 Auril. 2003. CIA:----- The Holland Queen crew roster lists a person named Hans Pakes as being aboard the Holland Queen. Pakes works in the ship's galley. From an Al Qaeda laptop computer captured in Afghanistan there is a roster of trainees in an explosives training unit in Kandahar, 1996. Abu al Masri, a Yemeni is listed on this roster. The record says he left for The Netherlands in January 1996. He is using the alias: Hans Pakes.

Report Date: 9 April, 2003. CIA -----[From a detainee at Guantanamo, Cuba who has given the name Abdul al Hada]:----- During an interview with CIA officers in March of 2003, al Hada is reported to have said:"In April 2003 there is not such good weather for flying". Asked what he meant by this statement, the detainee said: "That's for you to figure out". The detainee would not elaborate further.

Report Date 20 April. 2003. CIA:----- On 19 April, a car bomb was set off near the American Diplomatic Mission Headquarters in Buenos Aires. There were no casualties since the bomb seems to have gone off prematurely. The driver of the car carrying the bomb Survived. He has been identified as Jamil Musawi,who is known to be a member of Hezboliah. This organization has had a presence in several Latin American countries.

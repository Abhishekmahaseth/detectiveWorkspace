Report Date 26 April, 2003. Coast Guard intelligence to FBI and CIA:----- A routine reporting of ships bound for the USA. This report contains departure datelplace, destination, cargo manifest and crew roster and is required 96 hours before arrival in ports in the USA. Report from Amsterdam on 25 April, 2003 lists one container ship bound for the US, the Holland Queen, which is bound for Boston. Arrival time in Boston of the Holland Queen is 29 April, 1930hrs.

Report Date 27 April. 2003. FBI:----- A man named Carl Louis, whose residence is at 3410 Van Dyke Avenue in Detroit Michigan, was arrested by Detroit police for helping foreigners obtain Michigan drivers licenses using forged documents. Carl Louis is specifically charged with providing forged documents for a Lebanese national named Abdelhak Kherbane, who was arrested in connection with the attempted bombing on 2 April of the Beth Israel Synagogue in Detroit, Michigan.

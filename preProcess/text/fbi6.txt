Report Date 15, April 2003. FBI:----- Special report: Dr. Clark Adams, a Middle Eastern expert whose office is at the Pentagon, was last seen on 13 April, 2003 after he left his home at 1830hrs for the Home Depot store on Lee Highway in Merrifield. VA. Dr. Adams's family could give no explanation for his disappearance. The FBI is treating his disappearance as a possible case of abduction.

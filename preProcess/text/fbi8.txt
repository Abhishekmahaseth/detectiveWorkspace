Reprot Date 15 April. 2003. FBI:from INS: -----  Two men were arrested on 14 April, 2003 at Bush Intercontinental Airport in Houston, Texas. They are identified as Jamal Kalifa and Abul Hassan Salman. They both attempted to purchase airline tickets using Dutch passports that are believed to have been forged in Paris, France. The tickets they attempted to purchase were on Continental Airlines flight #458 from Bush Intercontinental in Houston to Reagan National Airport in Washington, DC.
